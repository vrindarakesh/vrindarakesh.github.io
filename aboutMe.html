Hey! I'm Vrinda.

I'm a budding UI designer and Front End Developer who loves to observe Nature and bring in it's vibrance into my designs.
I enjoy my time analysing the design projects/theme and creating illustrations, photographs and designing UI's that reflects its essence.
I also love coding and learning various new CSS concepts which helps to develop the whole story with the illustrations .

Away from screen, i love spending my time experimenting ways of natural farming and designing garden spaces.

Talking about my past professional experience, I started my career as Research Associate (Signal Processing) and later on moved to a startup (IoT) as Design Engineer to develop test and automation frameworks. I also had chance to design logos, Android UIs and brochures for the startup, which introduced me to open source design tools. Later on I had to take a break in my career for motherhood, which gave me a chance to explore my creative side.
